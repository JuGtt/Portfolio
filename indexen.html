Castertech Training

This project was created in Virtual Reality with the purpose of serving as training for new employees without putting them at risk.

It includes questions and situations that may occur in the workplace as well as information about protective equipment.

Marcopolo Configurator

This project is an interior bus configurator used by Marcopolo, aimed at prototyping the manufacturing stage.

I was responsible for implementing the entire seat selection system, material selection, and UI.

Moura Batteries

This project consists of an application used by Moura to assist in the assembly stage of batteries, using Augmented Reality to render the battery in its actual size on your phone screen with all the steps for assembly.

I worked on the battery recognition part using the Vuforia SDK, as well as the entire battery selection system and UI of the application.
